Packing & Delivery

After production cement is stored into the silo for packing. As per customer requirement, packing is done by packing machines which automatically fill the bags accurately 50kg. Then drop them to the delivery conveyor for direct delivery to truck, trawlers or cargo vessel. Rotary Auto Packing Machine confirm the accurate weight and then packed into the high quality poly propylene bags as per customer requirements. We have lot of delivery points and at the same time we can sufficient cement both to the road transport (truck & bulk carrier) & water transport (trawler, cargo). It should be noted that for the circumstances of our weather condition the quality of Anchor Cement remain unchanged for long time into these poly propylene or craft paper bags.
